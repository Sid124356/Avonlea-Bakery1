About

In the bustling community of Charlottetown, the capital of Prince Edward Island,
we proudly opened our bakery five years ago today. Inside our bakery, you'll be greeted
by the comforting aroma of freshly baked goods. We invite our customers to sit, study,
and socialize in our cozy space, enjoying a cup of one of our signature drinks alongside
a delicious baked treat.
